Nominations:

Nominations for this award from established researchers are welcome. Nominators should include (in a single pdf file):

  • (a) name and affiliation of nominee;
  • (b) a brief statement describing the nominee's accomplishments (limited to 1-2 pages);
  • (c) list of main publications of the nominee relevant to the award;
  • (d) name and affiliation of nominator.

The nominee should submit her/his application as described here.

Frequency:

The prize is awarded biannually at AMMCS meetings in Waterloo, Canada.

Deadline:

Applications can be submitted immediately after receiving a notification of abstract acceptance (decisions are communicated to all corresponding authors by April 15, 2013), but not later than August 12, 2013.

History:

The AMMCS Kolmogorov-Wiener Prize was established in 2011 at the International Conference on Applied Mathematics, Modeling and Computational Science held in Waterloo, Canada. Further details can be found here.

Contact Us:

By email to ammcs2013@wlu.ca with the subject line "AMMCS Kolmogorov-Wiener Young Researcher Award".
