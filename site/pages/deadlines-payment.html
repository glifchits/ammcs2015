Deadlines and Registration Fees

Registration for the conference is open: Please follow this link (TBA).

  • Deadline for minisymposia/special sessions proposals: January 10, 2015.
  • Deadline for submissions of abstracts: March 1, 2015.
  • Decision on abstracts: March 15, 2015.
  • Early Bird Registration: March 31, 2015.
  • Deadline for author registration: March 31, 2015.
  • Publication of full program: April 30, 2015.

Early bird registration fees are as follows:

  • Regular participant: $300
  • CAIMS member: $280
  • Postdoctoral fellow: $150
  • Professor Emeritus: $150
  • Students: $100
  • Accompanying person: $100
  • Conference banquet (to be held on Thursday): $70

{% set reg_deadline = "March 31, 2015" %}

Please note that:

  1. After {{ reg_deadline }} registration fees increase by $50 in each of the above four categories of participants.
  2. IMPORTANT: To be included in the program, all authors presenting their papers at the conference should register before the author registration deadline ({{ reg_deadline }}).
  3. For minisymposia/special session organizers and plenary speakers registration fees are waived.
  4. Full refund of registration fees can be made only prior to April 15, 2015 and 50% refund until April 30, 2015 (minus credit card surcharge, the conference treasurer can be contacted at t-ammcs2015@wlu.ca). No refund can be made after this date.
  5. Payments can be made by credit card. Please follow this link (TBA) to register and pay your registration fees.
