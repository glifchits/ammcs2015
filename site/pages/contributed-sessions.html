Contributed Sessions

A number of contributed sessions are organized consisting of contributed talks and poster presentations. These sessions cover a wide range of topics in applied mathematics, modeling and computational science, including emerging cross- discipline subject areas in mathematics, science, and engineering.

The complete list of contributed sessions will be available in March 2015. Topics of the contributed sessions currently include, but not limited to

Please submit your abstract via the AMMCS-CAIMS-2015 Management System. Each registered participant will obtain the AMMCS-CAIMS-2015 Book of Abstracts, along with the Program of the conference.

There will be also the AMMCS-CAIMS-2015 refereed proceedings, published after the conference. If you intend to submit your full conference paper to the proceedings, please go to the AMMCS-CAIMS-2015 Proceedings Pageand follow exactly the Author Instructions accessible from that page. Only the papers presented at the conference and accepted for publication through the referring process will be published in the AMMCS-CAIMS-2015 proceedings. Submission of full conference papers is not mandatory.

Extended versions of selected papers, presented at the conference, will be invited for several specialized scientific journals.
