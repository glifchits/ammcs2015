Proceedings

Proceedings of the AMMCS-2013 conference, based on refereed papers submitted by the registered participants, will be published by Springer.

All papers submitted for the Springer Proceedings of AMMCS-2013 will be peer reviewed. Only papers with at least one registered author will be considered. The papers should be prepared according to this Springer template (the complete zip file with Springer styles) and submitted to ammcs2013@wlu.ca by September 15, 2013. Your manuscript should not exceed 6 pages. Manuscripts that do not comply with the above requirements will be rejected without sending them to the reviewers.

In addition, extended versions of some selected papers, presented at the conference, will be invited for submission to the following journals:

  • Journal of Coupled Systems and Multiscale Dynamics;
  • Dynamics of Continuous, Discrete & Impulsive Systems: Series A;
  • Dynamics of Continuous, Discrete & Impulsive Systems: Series B;
  • Journal of Computational Science;
  • Nanoscale Systems: Mathematical Modeling, Theory and Applications.
